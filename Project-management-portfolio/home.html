Project Management Assignment: IT Infrastructure Best Practices

A comprehensive analysis of IT infrastructure best practices, governance frameworks, and real-world case studies. This assignment was submitted as part of the course requirements.

Best Practices, Real-World Examples, and Conclusion

ITIL and COBIT Frameworks

ITIL is a widely used IT Service Management (ITSM) framework that helps align IT services with business needs, improve reliability, and reduce costs. COBIT focuses on IT governance, risk management, and regulatory compliance. While ITIL supports service delivery, COBIT ensures oversight and control. Together, they offer a complete model for managing and governing IT.

Replication and Backup

Replication keeps multiple copies of data for service continuity, while backup allows data recovery after failure. Both are vital for minimizing downtime and managing risk.

Virtualization

Virtualization allows multiple virtual machines on a single physical server. Improving resource use, reducing hardware costs, and enabling scalable system deployment. The virtual machine administrator is responsible for allocating and managing system resources.

Cybersecurity

Effective cybersecurity means taking preventative measures against unauthorized access and cyber threats targeting IT infrastructure, such as firewalls, access controls, and other security measures. It is important to mitigate risks, support compliance, and ensure the integrity, security, and accessibility of information systems.

Monitoring

Continuous monitoring means conducting periodic system checks to monitor performance, detect performance issues, and ensure rapid response. Monitoring tools collect and analyze system metrics, such as system availability, performance, and resource utilization, to support quality assurance and operational stability.

Documentation and Training

Clear documentation and ongoing training help IT teams manage systems effectively, reduce errors, and support long-term reliability and knowledge sharing.

Real-World Examples

Netflix

Netflix migrated to Amazon Web Services (AWS) in 2008 to support streaming demands, gaining flexibility and scalability.

Hamad Bin Mohammed University

In 2020, Hamad Bin Mohammed University became the first Middle Eastern university to fully migrate its systems to AWS cloud infrastructure.

Conclusion

Strong IT infrastructure supports security, scalability, and business success. Using frameworks, planning best practices, and maintaining system knowledge ensures reliable, sustainable operations that help meet organizational goals.
